Hever Castle Campaigns

Hever Castle have a busy calendar of events, and while working at Hatched I was tasked with concepting, illustrating and laying out posters for their Tulip Celebrations and Hever in Bloom events.

The concept chosen by Hever focuses on the beauty of the gardens and the experience of being amongst nature and history.

The flowers are arranged to have larger, more detailed illustrations at the front which frame the castle behind. This gives the sense that when you’re looking at the posters, you’re there, experiencing it.
