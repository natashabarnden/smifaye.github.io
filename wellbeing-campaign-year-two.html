UPP - Year Two

The second year of UPP’s wellbeing campaign focuses on health, be that physical, mental or workplace-related.

We worked with illustrator Son of Alan to create a playful look-and-feel based on retro instructional graphics.

The use of instructional graphics highlighted that UPP were not asking anything that was impossible – and that the simplest things can make a big difference – it’s just about taking things one step at a time.
